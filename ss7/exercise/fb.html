We hope that you continue to enjoy the game on your mobile phone, where many more farm adventures await you. Thank you for being part of our awesome community; we look forward to seeing you in the Kingdom again soon! You can read more in our community forum: https://community.king.com/.../official.../p1... Please note that in-app purchases will be disabled on Facebook from February 24th, 2022.
